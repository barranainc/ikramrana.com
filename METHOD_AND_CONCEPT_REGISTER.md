# Method and Concept Register

Last updated: 2026-07-16

This register controls how named ideas are attributed across ikramrana.com. It reflects the owner's final review. Article titles may use plain language, but the body, metadata, schema, FAQs, and related links must not contradict these classifications.

## Ikram Rana methods and principles

1. **Decision Reduction Architecture**: Design AI workflows to reduce avoidable human decisions while routing consequential exceptions to a named person.
2. **Cognitive Load Budgeting**: Assess whether a workflow change reduces or increases the attention, switching, checking, and judgment required from people.
3. **Automation-Delegation Spectrum**: Place tasks on a spectrum from stable rule-based execution to work requiring context, judgment, and human responsibility.
4. **Context-First AI Adoption**: Assess the process, data, people, integrations, risks, and consequences before selecting an AI tool.
5. **Operational Reliability Threshold**: Determine when testing, monitoring, audit trails, and dependable exception handling support reducing continuous supervision for low-consequence routine work.
6. **Document Before You Automate**: Document steps, decisions, exceptions, assumptions, owners, and failure paths before automating a workflow.
7. **Judgment Quality Audit**: Test rules, criteria, assumptions, exceptions, potential harm, and escalation before consequential AI workflows go live.
8. **Strategic AI Minimalism**: Use fewer AI tools and concentrate effort on workflows with a clear problem, owner, measurement method, and operating fit.
9. **Accountability Architecture**: Assign system, process, decision, exception, and remediation ownership before deployment.
10. **Decision Commitment System**: Give a decision a named owner, a clear request, a proportionate deadline, a manageable set of options, and an escalation path.
11. **AI Appropriateness Matrix**: Evaluate a task using rule clarity, predictability, consequences, data sensitivity, and human accountability before selecting an AI role.
12. **Incentive-Aligned AI Adoption**: Check whether compensation, performance measures, recognition, and ownership support the intended workflow change.
13. **Capability Preservation Principle**: Preserve and periodically exercise human capabilities the business still needs when AI takes over part of the workload.

## Family and home-AI organization

Family AI Boundaries is a Real Life AI article concept, not part of the commercial business methodology. It covers household privacy, presence, relationships, developing capabilities, and decisions that should remain human.

Family content must:

- Remain a secondary category connected to Real Life AI.
- Avoid presenting educational, developmental, legal, medical, psychological, or safety advice as settled fact without reliable sources.
- Use illustrative labels when examples are hypothetical.
- Avoid guarantees about wellbeing, learning, anxiety, behaviour, or family outcomes.
