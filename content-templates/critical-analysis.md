# Critical AI Adoption Analysis

## Working title

Write the question or tension in plain business language.

## Direct answer

Answer the question in two or three sentences.

## What changed

State the new product, trend, regulation, behaviour, or claim. Include the relevant date.

## Why businesses should care

Explain the operational consequence without technical language.

## Ikram's position

State what is genuinely useful, what is overstated, what is missing, and what remains uncertain.

## Where AI may help

Use specific workflow examples.

## Where AI may make the problem worse

Cover added decisions, review work, poor data, weak ownership, privacy, security, or unreliable outputs as applicable.

## What must remain human

Name the decisions, approvals, exceptions, and escalation points.

## Practical next step

Give the reader one action they can take before buying or implementing a tool.

## Evidence

List primary and credible sources. Record access dates.
