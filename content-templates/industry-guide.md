# Industry Guide

## Business question

Use the words an owner or decision-maker in this industry would use.

## Direct answer

Explain the practical answer without technical language.

## How the work happens today

Describe the people, handoffs, tools, delays, repeated work, and judgment involved.

## Good uses of AI

List only workflows where the benefit and operating conditions are clear.

## Poor uses of AI

Explain where the task is too sensitive, ambiguous, low-volume, unstable, or dependent on human trust and judgment.

## Human decision points

Name who approves, who owns exceptions, and what must be recorded.

## Privacy, security, and regulatory considerations

Be specific to the industry and jurisdiction. Do not promise blanket compliance.

## Practical first workflow

Recommend a narrow starting point and explain why it is suitable.

## How to measure it

Use process measures such as completion time, correction rate, missed follow-ups, exception volume, adoption, and customer experience. Do not invent outcomes.
